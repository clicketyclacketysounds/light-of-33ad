The Light of 33 AD

Sunlight reflected off Earth in 33 AD, and what became of it.

Nearly 2,000 years ago, light bounced off our planet and never stopped moving. It is now an expanding shell almost 2,000 light-years wide. Four ways to explore where it went, and how much of Earth it still remembers.
